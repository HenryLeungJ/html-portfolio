Best Movies of All-Time

My top 3 movies of all-time

Megamind

Megamind is a story about a misunderstood super villian that was just not given the resources needed to become a superhero, thus he turned to a road of villany. This movie embodies struggles that happen in the real world in a cartoonish and outlandish way but still protrays the problems with impact.

The Incredibles

This movie is many people's childhood favourite. Superpowers, relatable heroes, and relatable villians. What more do you need?

Jujutsu Kaisen 0

I've been a big anime fan for quite a while now. Jujutsu Kaisen is a top tier anime in both plot and action scenes. This movie did wonders for the anime world.
